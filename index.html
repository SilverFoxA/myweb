What do we do

We create digital education solutions for children, without access to learning materials or access to low quality learning environments, that support their developmental journey in becoming independent thinkers and leaders can positively contribute to the world.

Our Project

We are developing open source software solutions that will facilitate autonomous learning environments for children living in remote areas of the world. Applying elements of gamification and machine learning, our 'intellifreind' software will enable children of aged 7 onwards to self learn literacy and numeracy.
